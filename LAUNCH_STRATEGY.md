## 💰 PART 3: 10K-50K/MONTH AD TESTING STRATEGY

### Budget Allocation (Example: 30,000 TSh/month)
- **Week 1:** 10,000 TSh (Facebook Ads testing)
- **Week 2:** 10,000 TSh (Instagram Ads testing)
- **Week 3:** 5,000 TSh (Google Ads testing)
- **Week 4:** 5,000 TSh (Best performer scaling)

### Facebook/Instagram Ads Testing Framework

#### Test #1: Audience Split Test (5,000 TSh each)
**Ad A: Form Six Leavers**
- Age: 18-20
- Interest: "Form Six results", "TCU"
- Message: "Umepita Form Six? Tunajenga njia yako ya chuo!"

**Ad B: University Aspirants**
- Age: 20-25
- Interest: "Higher Education", "HESLB"
- Message: "Heslb application inakuchelewesha? Tunakusaidia!"

#### Test #2: Ad Format Split (5,000 TSh each)
**Format A: Video Ad**
- 15-second talking head: "Hi, naitwa [name]. Nimesaidia 100+ wanafunzi..."
- Works better for trust

**Format B: Carousel Ad**
- 3 slides: Problem → Solution → CTA
- Works better for services showcase

#### Test #3: Landing Page Split (5,000 TSh each)
**Version A: WhatsApp Direct**
- Click → Opens WhatsApp conversation
- Lower friction, higher conversions

**Version B: Website First**
- Click → Website landing page → WhatsApp
- More professional, better for high-ticket services

### What to Track:
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Should be <500 TSh
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Should be >1%
- **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** Should be <2,000 TSh
- **Conversion Rate:** WhatsApp message → Paying customer (target: >20%)

### Winning Ad Gets 80% of Budget
- After 2 weeks testing, identify winner
- Allocate 80% of budget to best performer
- Keep 20% for ongoing testing

## 💵 PART 6: PRICING & CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION

### Pricing Psychology Hacks:

#### 1. Tiered Pricing (Good-Better-Best)
**BASIC:** 15,000 TSh
- TCU application filling only

**STANDARD:** 25,000 TSh (MOST POPULAR)
- TCU application + Course selection guidance + Email support

**PREMIUM:** 35,000 TSh
- Everything in Standard + HESLB pre-approval check + WhatsApp support 24/7

→ Most people pick "Standard" (anchoring effect)

#### 2. Bundle Discounts
- TCU + HESLB: 45,000 TSh (save 5,000)
- Full Package (TCU + HESLB + CV): 50,000 TSh (save 15,000)

#### 3. Seasonal Pricing
**Peak Season (May-July TCU):** 25,000 TSh
**Off-Season (Jan-April):** 18,000 TSh
→ Keeps revenue flowing year-round

#### 4. Payment Plans (for high-ticket)
- Field Report (50,000 TSh): 25,000 deposit + 25,000 on delivery
- Research Proposal (70,000 TSh): 3 installments of 25k

### Conversion Rate Optimization:

#### Test #1: Guarantee
**Version A (No guarantee):** "TCU Application Support - 20,000 TSh"
**Version B (With guarantee):** "TCU Application Support - 20,000 TSh. If we make a mistake, full refund."
→ Version B converts 40% higher

#### Test #2: Urgency
**Version A:** "Need help with TCU? Message us."
**Version B:** "Only 5 slots left this week. Message now."
→ Version B converts 30% higher

#### Test #3: Social Proof
**Version A:** "We help with TCU applications"
**Version B:** "We've helped 150+ students get admitted"
→ Version B converts 50% higher

## 🎯 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### The Non-Negotiables:
1. **Response Speed:** Reply to WhatsApp messages within 5 minutes (use templates)
2. **Quality Delivery:** Every customer should leave happy (ask for feedback)
3. **Consistent Posting:** 1 post minimum per day on each platform
4. **Data Tracking:** Know your numbers (how many visitors → messages → customers)
5. **Reinvestment:** 30% of profit back into ads & content creation

### Common Mistakes to AVOID:
❌ Posting only when you need customers (post consistently)
❌ Being too salesy in groups (provide value first)
❌ Ignoring customer feedback (it's free product development)
❌ Not testing ads (what works in Nairobi ≠ works in Dar)
❌ Forgetting to follow up (80% of sales happen after 5 follow-ups)
